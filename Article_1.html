LEARN HOW TO PRONOUNCE THESE NAMES, PEOPLE!

Seriously, unless you want to sound like a dipshit. December 11, 2016

If you're even going to pretend that you "like fashion" or are "fashionable," please, for the love of god, learn how to pronounce these words correctly because I swear if I hear "mo-chine-oh" one more time I'm going to lose it.

With the help of my *friends* at Harper's Bazaar, here is an acceptable array of high fashion brands along with their pronunciation guide. Do me a favor and memorize these, alright? I've even included a few audio clips of me actually saying the names so you have absolutely no excuse to sound like a nitwit ever again. You're welcome.
